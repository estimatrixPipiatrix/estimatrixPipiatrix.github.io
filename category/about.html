About

Published: Sun 17 July 2022
By Kayla Lewis

In about.

Hello, I'm Kayla Lewis, a professor in the New York City area who uses critical systems thinking, ideas from the behavioral sciences, and machine learning to support people in their decision making.

It's important to emphasize that I don't tell anyone what they should do. Instead, I use a structured approach, with time-tested methodologies, to facilitate them making their own choices.

Herein I write about how we can use such an approach to improve the quality of our decisions.
